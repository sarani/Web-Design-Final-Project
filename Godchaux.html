Keith and Donna Godchaux

Keith Godchaux aided the dead when they finally faced the fact that they needed a keyboard player. Pigpen was too frail, and with his health declining, they needed someone to vill the void. One day a woman, Donna Jean Godchaux, stopped Garcia at a Keysone Korner show in San Fransico and introduced her husband Keith. After playing with Garcia, and after the complete band, Godchaux became the new piano player, and Donna a backup vocalist. He was brilliant for the first three or so years he played with the band, but sensitive and prone to depression, the road ate him alive and he and wife Donna were asked to leave the band.
